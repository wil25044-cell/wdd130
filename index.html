About Me

I believe clothing is more than fabric—it’s expression, innovation, and identity. As a student at BYU–Idaho majoring in Apparel Entrepreneurship, I’m exploring how fashion design, business, and creativity blend together to create something new.

I’m developing the skills to design, manage, and launch successful apparel ventures. I’m especially interested in sustainable fashion, design, and small business ownership. I’m eager to keep learning and applying the skills I’m learning in real-world settings.

My goal is to own a business in fashion design and production, and combine creativity with strategy to bring innovative apparel ideas to life. I’m excited to connect with professionals in fashion, retail, and entrepreneurship who share a passion for creativity and the apparel industry. I’m always open to learning opportunities, internships, and collaborations.
